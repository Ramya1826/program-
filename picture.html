Sudha Murty

Sudha Murty is an Indian educator, author and philanthropist who is chairperson of the Infosys Foundation. She is married to the co-founder of Infosys, N. R. Narayana Murty. Murty was awarded the Padma Shri, the fourth highest civilian award in India, for social work by the Government of India in 2006.

Born in 1950 in Shiggaon, India, Dr Sudha Murthy is an extraordinary Indian author and renowned philanthropist. She has contributed to society in many ways. An example of Dr Sudha Murthy's contribution to society includes co-founding the Infosys Foundation in 1996 with her husband.
